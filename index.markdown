Hi! My name is Reece Villarin Akana and I am passionate about bio and AI. I did my PhD in Cancer Biology at Stanford in the Jerby Lab studying genetic regulators of tumor cell sensitivity to CD8+ T cell killing. While there I gained appreciation in the power of AI to be used as a tool to assist in bio research. Currently my biggest interests are in LLMs for hypothesis generation/tool use (such as [Biomni](https://biomni.stanford.edu/)) or DNA language models (such as [Evo2](https://arcinstitute.org/tools/evo)).  

In my free time I like to [boulder](https://www.instagram.com/reeceontherocks/) and play [piano](https://on.soundcloud.com/FUCcZmj0OiZbpGBO1p).
